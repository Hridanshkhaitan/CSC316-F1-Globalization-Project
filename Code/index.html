F1 Going Global

F1 races used to be primarily held in Europe but has been aiming to cast a wider net as time goes on, which can be seen in the decline of the number of races hosted in Europe

[1]

In 2017, Liberty Media became the owner of the Formula One brand and this could be part of different management decisions with respect to race schedules and prices.

Unfortunately, ticket prices for attending F1 races have gone up significantly. While a lot of race video is available online and live races are available through both streaming services and cable networks, the in-person experience is hard to replicate from home for many fans.
